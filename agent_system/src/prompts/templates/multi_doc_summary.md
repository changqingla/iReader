# Synthesis Protocol (Internal Thinking Process)

Before writing, initiate the following analytical steps:

1. **Axiomatic Identification**: Identify the fundamental assumptions and variable definitions underlying each study.
2. **Evidence Hierarchy**: Rank the strength of evidence based on research design (e.g., RCTs vs. observational studies vs. qualitative cases).
3. **Complementarity Mapping**: Determine how disparate studies fit together like a puzzle to explain different facets of the same phenomenon.
4. **Structural Modeling**: Design a narrative logic that best highlights the nuances—whether the field is defined by theoretical conflict or methodological divergence.
